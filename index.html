Biography

I am Satyam Kumar, currently working as a research assistant at Intelligent systems laboratory IIT Kanpur under the supervision of Prof. Laxmidhar Behera. My interest is to decode the information content of neural signals and further its use in Brain-computer interfaces (BCI). I recently defended my master’s thesis on Motor Imagery BCI. In summer 2016, I was fortunate enough to receive Charpak Scholarship for research internship under Prof. Francesco P. Andriulli at Telecom Bretagne, France. During My master’s thesis, I visited the Rehabilitation Engineering Laboratory at ETH Zurich and worked with Mr. Oliver Bichsel under the supervision of Prof. Roger Gassert. Currently, I am supervising undergraduate students on transfer learning and parallel working on Riemannian geometry based approaches in BCI.

Apart from my academic endeavors, I am a potterhead and love to play soccer as central defensive midfielder.
